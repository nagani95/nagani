# Nagani Asset Drop-In Checklist

This folder stores future production assets for Nagani Traditional.

Do not use emoji as final assets.
Do not use western slot-machine style.
Do not mix random art styles.

All assets should follow:

- Premium Myanmar traditional feeling
- Red dragon identity
- Dark lacquer / gold / jade color direction
- Mobile-readable shapes
- High resolution
- Transparent PNG/WebP where useful
- Consistent lighting and angle
- Not childish, not cheap cartoon
